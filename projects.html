Our Featured Projects

Detailed overview of our key initiatives and their impact on communities across Kenya.

Ongoing

Poultry Waste-to-Compost Pilot Project

Transforming poultry manure from a pollutant into valuable organic fertilizer for smallholder farmers in Kiambu County. This pilot project aims to establish a sustainable model for agricultural waste management.

Kiambu County
Dec 2025 - Jun 2026
150+ Farmers
  • Safe manure collection and handling training
  • Composting demonstration centers
  • Free compost distribution to farmers
  • Environmental pollution reduction

Planning Phase

Clean Energy Briquette Production Initiative

Developing affordable, clean cooking fuel from agricultural waste to reduce deforestation and indoor air pollution while creating income opportunities for youth and women.

Nairobi & Kajiado Counties
Mar 2026 - Dec 2026
50 Youth/Women
  • Briquette production training
  • Small-scale production units
  • Market linkage development
  • Clean energy awareness campaigns
