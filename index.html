Our Key Focus Areas

Poultry Waste Management

Promoting safe collection, treatment, and utilization of poultry and organic waste to enhance soil fertility and reduce pollution.

Climate-Smart Agriculture

Training farmers in regenerative farming practices to enhance food security and strengthen climate adaptation.

AMR Awareness & One Health
