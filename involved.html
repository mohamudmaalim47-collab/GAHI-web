Partnership Opportunities

We welcome collaborations with organizations, institutions, and businesses that share our commitment to sustainable agriculture and community health.

Corporate Partnerships

Partner with GAHI through corporate social responsibility (CSR) initiatives, employee volunteering programs, or cause-related marketing campaigns. Benefits include positive brand association, employee engagement, and measurable social impact.

Academic & Research Collaborations

Collaborate on research projects, student internships, field studies, or joint publications. We work with universities and research institutions on topics ranging from waste management to antimicrobial resistance and climate-smart agriculture.

NGO & CBO Networks

Join our network of community-based organizations and non-profits working on sustainable development. Share resources, coordinate interventions, and amplify our collective impact through joint advocacy and programming.

Government & Agency Collaborations

Work with county and national government agencies on policy advocacy, program implementation, and community development initiatives aligned with national priorities like the Big Four Agenda and Vision 2030.
